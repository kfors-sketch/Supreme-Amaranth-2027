Rules & Tips
  • ID: lowercase letters, numbers, and dashes only (e.g. sunflower-pendant).
  • Type: fixed (simple item), qty (choose quantity), amount (open dollar amount / donation-style), variantQty (choose from labeled variants with quantity, e.g. 1 for $10, 3 for $25, 6 for $40).
  • Price: base price (USD). For variantQty, use the Variants box instead of Price.
  • Shipping & Handling: per-item amount (USD). Checkout charges the highest shipping once per order.
  • Publish Start/End: local date/time (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM) for when the product is live/closed.
  • Chair Emails: optional, comma-separated list for reporting.
  • Report Frequency: controls how often chair reports are sent for this product.
  • Additional Images: put one URL per line for extra gallery images.
  • Year-over-year Slot: required label (e.g. Session Coin) to group similar products across years.
  • Sort Order: lower numbers show first (admin + public).
